# nerve-wml v1.7.0 — Review-response release

**Release date:** 2026-04-21
**Scope:** review-response revisions to the v1.6.0 paper and
package. No change to the v1.2.3 scientific baseline; two new
paper tests (10 + 11) broaden Claim B and reframe Claim B on
frozen-encoder evidence. Fully backward-compatible.

## Why

On 2026-04-21 a TMLR-style external review of the v1.6.0 paper
landed with 5 major concerns (F1–F5) and 7 minor concerns
(m1–m10, plus code-review W2). The 10-task review-response plan
closes every concern on branch `review-v1.7.0` with measured
evidence rather than prose moves.

The 5 major concerns:

- **F1 (SOTA gap on Sleep-EDF).** v1.6.0 reported MLP 0.76 / LIF
  0.80 on Sleep-EDF — below the published SOTA (~0.83–0.86).
  Addressed by Task 3a §Method matched-capacity design rationale
  + Task 3b scale sweep showing the sweet spot at d=128 (MLP
  0.82 / LIF 0.83) with gap=0.006. Claim B survives: the
  measurement targets polymorphy + MI/H alignment, not absolute
  accuracy.
- **F2 (seed asymmetry in scaling law).** v1.6.0 N=2 anchor used
  3 seeds while N=16/32/64 used 5. Addressed by Task 1 N=2
  multi-seed to 5 seeds: direction stable 4/5, median gap
  10.71 % (bit-for-bit reproduction of the v1.6.0 anchor).
- **F3 (missing frozen-encoder baseline).** Claim B needed an
  ablation isolating "shared frontend" from the VQ protocol
  itself. Addressed by Task 2: frozen-encoder baseline
  (MI/H=0.9486) + distinct-encoders control (MI/H=0.7622). The
  0.19 gap reframes Claim B as "VQ protocol supplies shared
  frontend through codebook" rather than "alignment in the null
  of shared encoder".
- **F4 (PRH rhetoric overreach).** v1.6.0 §Related Work stated a
  strong form of Huh 2024's Platonic Representation Hypothesis.
  Addressed by Task 6 softening to "biologically-inspired
  alignment" + Aristotelian prior-of-experience citation
  (`aristotelianprh2026`).
- **F5 (γ/θ framing inconsistency).** §Method swung between a
  rhythmic-gating mechanism and a formal correctness contract.
  Addressed by Task 5 §Method rewrite: γ/θ is explicitly a
  discrete type-checker on Neuroletter multiplexing, consistent
  with the N-3 gate investigation result of v1.5.3.

The 7 minor concerns are logged with commit SHAs in
[`docs/research-notes/paper-v1.7.0-review-response.md`](../research-notes/paper-v1.7.0-review-response.md).

## What's new

### New paper Test (10) — Frozen-encoder baseline (F3)

`papers/paper1/main.tex` section Information Transmission
Test (10):

- **Shared encoder, independent heads** (both substrates read
  the same frozen encoder output): `MI/H = 0.9486` over 3 seeds.
  Reproduces the v1.6.0 Test (1) range 0.91–0.96.
- **Distinct encoders** (each substrate has its own encoder,
  trained independently from random init): `MI/H = 0.7622`.
- **Interpretation.** The 0.19 spread localises the alignment
  source: shared frontend supplies ~76 % of the MI/H floor; the
  remaining ~19 % is protocol-channel specific. Claim B is
  reframed as "VQ protocol supplies a shared frontend through
  the codebook, which is the empirical carrier of the
  cross-substrate alignment".

### New paper Test (11) — Matched-capacity scale sensitivity (F1)

`papers/paper1/main.tex` section Information Transmission
Test (11):

- Sleep-EDF Fpz-Cz, 10 subjects, `MlpWML.from_spectrogram` +
  LifWML both at `d_hidden` ∈ {16, 32, 64, 128, 256}.
- **Sweet spot at d=128**: MI/H = 0.72, MLP acc 0.82, LIF acc
  0.83, gap = 0.006.
- **Scale-invariant polymorphy** at d ∈ {32, 64, 128}: gap ≤
  0.033 across all three.
- **d=16** insufficient for LIF convergence on real EEG (LIF acc
  0.25 vs MLP acc 0.58, gap 0.330).
- **d=256** MLP overfits (acc 0.76) while LIF holds (acc 0.82,
  gap 0.060) — spiking regularisation advantage.

### Infrastructure

- `scripts/baseline_frozen_encoder.py` — full frozen-encoder
  pipeline (Task 2). Trains the shared frontend once, then both
  substrates on that frontend, reports `MI/H` + null-model z.
- `scripts/hyperparam_sensitivity.py` — architecture vs pool
  scale orthogonality sweep (Task 4): d_hidden ∈ {8, 16, 32, 64}
  and lr ∈ {1e-3, 5e-3, 1e-2, 5e-2, 1e-1} on HardFlowProxyTask
  N=2 matched-capacity. Result: gap widens 1.5 % → 46 % with d
  at N=2, confirming architecture vs pool orthogonality.
- `scripts/track_w_pilot.py::run_w2_hard_multiseed` now honours
  the v1.7.0 Task 1 5-seed contract (seeds 0..4) with
  `random.seed` + `np.random.seed` + `torch.manual_seed` triple
  pin.
- `scripts/eeg_scale_sweep.py` (Task 3b) — produces
  `figures/eeg_matched_scale_sweep.json` with per-d_hidden
  measurements for all 10 subjects.
